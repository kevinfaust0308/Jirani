A peer-to-peer microlending Blockchain platform

Blockchain increases transparency and significantly reduces interest rates. It does so because of its decentralized nature, meaning that it can assist several microfinancing bodies without the need for mediators or central authorities. Furthermore, smart contracts make repaying these loans easier by creating a bond based on the terms and conditions specified for repayment.
